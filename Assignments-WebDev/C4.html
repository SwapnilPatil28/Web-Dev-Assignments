Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • ½ cup fresh cream
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 10-12 cashews (soaked & ground into paste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and 1 tbsp butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and bay leaf, let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Mix in cashew paste and stir well. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add ½ cup water and let the gravy simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Add paneer cubes, green chilies, and garam masala. Mix gently.
  8. Stir in cream and remaining butter. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Enjoy your hot Paneer Butter Masala!!!
